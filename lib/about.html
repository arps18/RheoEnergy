ABOUT US




Dynamic and Independent Partnership

Rheos Energy Solutions is a consolidated Indian company.

We have a dynamic and independent partnership with globally renown brands like Aerzen, IGEMA Steam & Boiler and Sodimate for sales and supply range of products to meet the customer’s requirement of engineering solutions and services, market and support related product and develop end-user solutions in partnership with various global manufacturers.

Rheos Energy operates in the Oil & Gas, Petrochemical and Power-Generation industries through three different business Units. The company is located in the industrial area of western India and covers businesses of India in the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia


Committed to the continuous development and realization of solutions aimed at meeting increasingly demanding technical requirements, Rheos Energy has an ethical and responsible customer-driven business approach which is aimed at continuously providing the product with highest performance and reliability and with a strong customer focus support as well.

The manufacturing activities are organized in three factories over a covered area of 80.000 sq. ft. a staff of over 25 people. All phases of component manufacturing and parts engineering, including the process and mechanical design of it as well as manufacturing, testing, painting, and packing are carried out in-house.

We bring together, in whatever combination is required, any of our product lines and service offerings. The result is a single, complete solution comprising custom engineering, equipment design, facility planning, construction, equipment commissioning and operation.
