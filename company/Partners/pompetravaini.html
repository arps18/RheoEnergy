Pompetravaini is in busines since 1929. At Pompetravaini we design, manufacture, assembly and test centrifugal pumps (ISO 2858 and ISO 5199), liquid ring vacuum pumps and compressors, blowers and boosters. In the centrifugal range we can offer multistage for high pressure, low boiling point gases multistage, hot oil pump. Pompetravaini is an international group exporting in over 80 countries with a network of subsidiaries and exclusive distributors.
