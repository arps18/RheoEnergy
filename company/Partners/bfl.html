BFL


↵ MANUFACTURER'S WEBSITE

In the global hydropower market, small hydro is emerging as the future. BFL is a world leader in the small hydro arena, having cultivated an extremely satisfied global customer base. We have accomplished this by adhering to a simple ideology: Relationship matters.

BFL also builds concern for the environment in to its plants, by using technology that harness the power of nature, without causing harm.

BFL is one of the largest and fastest-growing providers of water-to-wire, turnkey solutions, delivering small hydroplants with outputs ranging from 0.5 megawatts to 25 megawatts. We provide equipment and total service, from machine selection to power plant commissioning, working in true partnership with our customers every step of the way.

With growth fueled by our laser-like focus on customer satisfaction, over 100 BFL hydropower solutions dot every continent of the world (except Antarctica). Almost 50% of our projects are repeat business, with customer referrals driving another 25% of revenues.
